📋 How to Apply — Step by Step

Complete these 5 steps to successfully book your hostel room

1
Create Account

2
Fill Application

Complete the room application form with your personal details, preferences, and required documents.

3
Choose Room Type

4
Pay Registration Fee

5
Confirmation

Receive your room allotment letter via email within 3–5 working days after admin verification.

📜 Hostel Rules & Regulations

All residents must adhere to the following rules. Violations may result in disciplinary action.

Entry Curfew

All students must return to the hostel by 10:00 PM. Late entry requires prior written permission from the warden.

No Alcohol or Smoking

Consumption or possession of alcohol, tobacco, or any illegal substances on hostel premises is strictly prohibited.

Noise & Silence Hours

Maintain silence between 10:00 PM – 6:00 AM. Loud music or noise disturbances are not permitted at any time.

No Unauthorized Visitors

Visitors are only allowed in designated common areas between 10:00 AM and 7:00 PM with valid ID.

Room Cleanliness

Residents must keep their rooms and shared spaces clean. Rooms are subject to weekly inspection by hostel staff.

ID Card Mandatory

Students must carry their hostel ID card at all times. Lost cards must be reported immediately to the office.

Electrical Appliances

Only approved appliances (laptop, phone charger) are permitted. Hotplates and high-wattage devices are banned.

Fee Payment Deadline

Monthly hostel fees must be paid by the 5th of each month. Late payment attracts a penalty of $10 per week.

Property Damage

Any damage to hostel property must be reported and the cost of repairs will be deducted from the security deposit.

Checkout Notice

A minimum 15-day written notice is required before vacating the hostel. Security deposit is refunded within 30 days.

Fire Safety

Familiarize yourself with emergency exits and fire assembly points. Tampering with fire equipment is a serious offence.

Respectful Conduct

Bullying, ragging, discrimination, or harassment of any form will result in immediate expulsion from the hostel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply for a hostel room?
Any currently enrolled student with a valid student ID from the partner institution is eligible to apply for hostel accommodation.
How long does the allotment process take?
After submitting a complete application, allotment confirmation is typically issued within 3–5 working days, subject to room availability.
Is the registration fee refundable?
The registration fee is non-refundable. However, the security deposit is fully refundable upon checkout, subject to room condition inspection.
Can I request a specific roommate?
Yes, roommate preferences can be submitted in the application form. We will do our best to accommodate requests based on availability.
What documents are required?
You'll need: valid student ID, admission letter, passport-size photo, parent/guardian contact details, and a medical fitness certificate.
How do I track my application status?
Log in to the Student Portal using your credentials. Your application status updates in real-time on your dashboard.
